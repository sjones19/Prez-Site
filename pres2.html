Presidential Election Info

The presidential election runs on a cycle, which leads up to the actual voting day. The first part starts in the spring when the announce that they will be running for president, a year before voting day. Next comes a year before the voting day around the summer in which the primaries and caucuses start their debates. Around January to June of the election year states and parties hold the primaries and caucuses. Parties start holding nominating conventions in order to choose their candidates. This normally takes place through July into early September. September and October is the start of the candidates participating in Presidential debates. In early November is election day, which then leads to the electors casting their votes into the Electoral College. The year after the election around early January congress counts the electoral votes and then January 20th is Inauguration day.

Donald J. Trump

Donald John Trump, is an known by many as an American businessman and a huge television personality. Besides being the host of his own hit TV series, like the well know "The Apprentice", he is also an author and politician. At the age of 70, Donald is a nominee of the Republican Party for the 2016- 2017 Presidential election.

Born: June 14, 1946, Queens, New York City, NY

Education: Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania (1968)

Spouses: Melania Trump (m. 2005), Marla Maples (m. 1993 till 1999), Ivana Trump (m. 1977 till 1992)

Children: Ivanka Trump, Tiffany Trump, Barron Trump, Eric Trump, Donald Trump Jr.

Hilary Clinton

Hillary Diane Rodham Clinton, age sixty-eight, is the nominee for the Democratic Party this 2016-2017 Presidential year. Hilary has had many experiences with working with politics, granted she was the First Lady from 1993-2001, the 67th United States Secretary of State from 2009-2013, and the junior United States Senator from 2001-2009.

Born: October 26, 1947

Education: Wellesley College and Yale Law School with postgraduate classes at Yale Child Study Center

Spouses: Bill Clinton (m. 1975)

Children: Chelsea Clinton
